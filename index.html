Welcome to Hextract

You are the captain of a small extraction team sent to investigate the remains of a derelict vessel floating the expanse of space.
Your crew are your only resource this far out in the expanse, aslong as your have atleast one crew member you can complete your mission.

To play: Choose from the two options below. After you select your option, the dice will roll. You must roll a three or higher to succeed.

Every decision you make could mean life of death for your crew.
Can you get everyone out alive?
